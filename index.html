This Privacy Policy explains how Chrono Clash ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile game application.

Chrono Clash is fully playable offline. All your game data stays on your device. When you have internet connectivity, we may show ads to support the game's development.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game Data (Stored Locally Only)

üíæ Your Device, Your Data

All game-related data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Game Progress: Your current level, completed stages, and achievements
  • Player Statistics: Your scores, rankings, and accumulated points
  • Hero Data: Your selected heroes, their levels, health, and energy
  • Game Settings: Audio preferences, difficulty settings, and display options
  • Tutorial Status: Whether you've completed the game tutorial
  • Move History: Recent moves for the rewind ability (temporary storage)

1.2 Advertising Data (When Connected to Internet)

üì∫ Advertising Information

When you have internet connectivity, our advertising partners may collect limited data to show you relevant ads and support the game's development.

When you're connected to the internet, advertising networks may collect:

  • Advertising ID: Your device's advertising identifier (can be reset in device settings)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, screen size for ad compatibility
  • IP Address: Your internet connection's IP address for geographic ad targeting
  • Ad Interaction: Whether you view, click, or interact with advertisements
  • App Usage: Basic app usage patterns to determine appropriate ad timing

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Game Functionality (Offline)

  • Save Progress: Store your game advancement locally on your device
  • Personalize Experience: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Enable Features: Support time abilities, hero management, and achievements
  • Maintain Rankings: Calculate and display your player rank

2.2 Advertising (Online)

  • Show Relevant Ads: Display advertisements that may interest you
  • Support Development: Generate revenue to maintain and improve the game
  • Optimize Ad Experience: Ensure ads are appropriate for your device and region
  • Measure Performance: Track ad effectiveness (anonymized data only)

4. Your Advertising Choices

4.1 Opt-Out Options

  • Device Settings: Reset your advertising ID or limit ad tracking in your device settings
  • Play Offline: Disconnect from internet to avoid all advertising data collection
  • Ad Preferences: Manage your ad preferences through Google Ad Settings or similar services

4.2 How to Limit Ad Tracking

On Android:

  • Go to Settings > Google > Ads
  • Turn on "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • Reset your advertising ID if desired

On iOS:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
  • Turn off "Personalized Ads"
  • You can also limit tracking in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

5.2 Advertising Data Security

  • Partner Standards: Advertising partners follow industry security standards
  • Limited Collection: Only necessary data for ad serving is collected
  • Secure Transmission: All ad-related data is transmitted securely
